CALL FOR PAPERS

SPICE is the premier forum for the presentation of new advances and research results in the field of Civil Engineering. The conference will bring together leading academic scientists, researchers and scholars in the domain of interest from around the world.
Topics pertaining to the scope of the conference will include, but are not limited to :

STRUCTURAL

  • Advances in Structural Engineering
  • Advanced / Hybrid Construction
  • Materials
  • Green Building Concepts
  • Seismic Resistant Structures
  • FEM Techniques in Civil Engineering
  • Precast Construction Techniques
  • Sustainable Structures
  • Low Cost Solutions
  • Waste Utilization

GEOTECHNICAL

  • Ground Improvement Techniques
  • Geo-environmental Engineering
  • Soil Structure Interaction Studies
  • Dynamic Behavior of Soils

WATER RESOURCES & ENVIRONMENTAL

  • Coastal Management & Climate Change Irrigation Structures
  • Application of Remote Sensing and GIS
  • E-Waste & Solid Waste Management
  • Environmental Issues & Solutions
  • Advanced Wastewater Treatment
  • Groundwater Improvement Techniques

TRANSPORTATION

  • Geomatics & Transportation
  • Sustainable Infrastructure
  • Modern Transportation Systems
  • Intelligent Transportation Systems
  • Traffic Signals using ITS
